Everbridge 360™

Everbridge 360™ is a unified enterprise resilience platform for all your organizational roles. It is designed to provide an integrated, intuitive experience across all Everbridge product lines.

Everbridge 360 delivers:

  • Faster, more accurate responses to external and internal threats.
  • An easier way to manage what's important—your people and assets.
  • Enhanced resilience to your organization, helping you be proactive rather than reactive.
  • Enterprise-class reliability and scale.
  • An open, extensible platform.

Everbridge 360 includes the following features:

  • Event Types with predetermined event categories based on risk intelligence feeds plus the ability for your organization to create custom event types. Event types link together events, alerts, and templates, decreasing the mean time to communicate.
  • A communications workflow that combines the best of both worlds from the notifications and incident communications functionality with the addition of feature enhancements that help optimize the launch process, lessen training time, and reduce the opportunity for errors.

Toggle on Everbridge 360

To toggle on Everbridge 360 in your instance, an administrator in your organization needs to enable it (this is found under Settings > Organization > Everbridge 360 > Default Settings. Once enabled, you can toggle on Everbridge 360 while still retaining access to existing modules like Notifications and Incidents.

**image**

There are a few considerations to keep in mind when using the toggle as an Admin:

  1. The Admin's user-level toggle at the Account level will be OFF by default. Unlike at the Organization level, there is no Account-level setting that controls the visibility of the user-level toggle at the Account level.
  2. The option in Organization Settings controls the visibility of the toggle at the Organization level, which is OFF by default. All Organization Admins can access this Setting irrespective of the status of the Account-level toggle.
  3. If the first is enabled by a user and the second is disabled by the Organization Admin, then the left side menu will only be displayed at the Account level for the respective user.
  4. If the first is enabled by a user and the second is enabled by the Organization Admin, then the left side menu will be displayed at both the Account and Organization levels for the respective user. Toggle preference will persist at both the Account and Organization levels.
