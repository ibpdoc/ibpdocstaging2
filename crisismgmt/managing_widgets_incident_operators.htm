Managing Widgets (Incident Operator)

Incident Operators can manage widgets on a Dashboard by adding or removing different items to fit their needs.

Manage Widgets

To add or remove widgets:

  1. From the Critical Event Home page, look at the Dashboard.
  2. In the Active Events section, select the Critical Event you want to update.
  3. Click the desired name of the Single Event Dashboard. 
  4. From the Actions menu, select Manage Dashboard. The Manage Dashboard dialog appears.
  5. Update your widgets as needed.
    • Select the widgets that will be available to the viewers of this dashboard, including yourself.
    • Clear the checkboxes of the widgets you do not want on this dashboard.
  6. Click Save.
